API'S

✔ 1)register employer  (api/Auth/register/employer)(POST)(All)

✔ 2)register candidate (api/Auth/register/candidate)(POST)(All)

✔ 3)login user (api/Auth/login)(POST)(All)

✔ 4)Read Users(GET)(Admin)
	~ get employers (api/get/employers) (GET)
	~ get candidates (api/get/candidates) (GET)

✔ 5)Create Job (api/create/job)(POST)(Employer)

✔ 6)Read All Job (api/get/job)(GET)(Admin, Candidate)

✔ 7)Read Own Created Jobs (api/get/created-jobs)(GET)(Employer)

✔ 8)soft Delete Job (api/delete/job)(PUT)(Employer)

✔ 9)Apply Job(POST)(Candidates)

✔ 10)View own Job Application History (api/get/applications-by-candidate)(GET)(Candidate)

✔ 11)Read All Application (api/get/applications)(GET)(Admin)

12)view all applied candidates in particular job (GET)(Employer)

✔ 13)jobs by employer (api/get/jobs-by-employer)(GET)(Admin)

14)Update status of applied applications on job (PUT)(Employer)
